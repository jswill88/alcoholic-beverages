IPA

IPA stands for India Pale Ale. It was invented in Britain. They are bitter and hoppy. There are a variety of different types such as New England and West Coast. Some famous brands are Lagunitas, Dogfish Head, and Sierra Nevada. They go well with sausage or French Fries.

Lager

The Lager was invented in early 19th century Bavaria. They are based on Pilsner stlye beers. They are only about 3-5% alcohol by volume. Some famous lager brands are Corona, Budweiser, and Stella Artois. They go well with chicken and light pasta.
